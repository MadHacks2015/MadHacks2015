About

What

MadHacks 2015 is UW-Madison's first intercollegiate hackathon. Come make history with 500 of your peers in one of the Midwest's greatest collaborative events this spring.

When

Opening ceremonies will commence at 5pm on April 17th and project demos will conclude by noon on April 19.

Where

MadHacks will be hosted at 605 Science Drive, Madison, WI.

Who

You! If you're a student at a US college, you should apply to MadHacks to come show off your talents!

How

You'll spend 36 hours hacking on a new project in teams of 1-4. After projects have been completed, you'll demo your project to the other hackers. May the maddest project win!

About

What

MadHacks 2015 is UW-Madison's first intercollegiate hackathon. Come make history with 500 of your peers in one of the Midwest's greatest collaborative events this spring.

When

Opening ceremonies will commence at 5pm on April 17th and project demos will conclude by noon on April 19.

Where

MadHacks will be hosted at 605 Science Drive, Madison, WI.

Who

You! If you're a student at a US college, you should apply to MadHacks to come show off your talents!

How

You'll spend 36 hours hacking on a new project in teams of 1-4. After projects have been completed, you'll demo your project to the other hackers. May the maddest project win!

What's a Hackathon?

If you've never had the pleasure of attending a hackathon before, our friends and sponsors at Major League Hacking have put together a great intro about what a hackathon is and why you should attend one!

What makes MadHacks different?

Unlike other hackathons, MadHacks is going to be putting emphasis on the wacky, the original, and the downright impossible! Like the mad-genius computer scientists before us, we want to test our boundaries like never before! We're reaching out to the highly dedicated risk-takers, the envelope pushers, and the daredevils. We hope to produce a new kind of hackathon: where the winners are chosen by the hackers, and the sponsors feel like they are right in the middle of the hacker community itself — rather than on the outside looking in.

Fequently Asked Questions

If you've got a burning question that isn't addressed here, shoot us an email at team@madhacks.org !

Will there be buses or travel reimbursement?

We're waiting for everyone to sign up to get a feel for the transport demand. It's likely that we'll be sending buses along the major routes for nearby (Midwest) colleges. If you don't wind up with a bus, we'll reimburse up to $50 in travelling expenses per person (can you say carpool party anyone?).

What should I bring?

Whatever you need to make your hack a success! For most people, this means a laptop, charger, smartphone, and your friends! Optional but nice things include a pillow, a change of clothes, and a toothbrush and toothpaste. Please don't bring anything you wouldn't take on a school field trip — no drugs or weapons.

If I drive, where can I park?

We'll have more information about parking as the date of the event approaches.

What's the food situation?

MadHacks will be catered with enough food to keep you hacking well into the night, thanks to our wonderful sponsors.
